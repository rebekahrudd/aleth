    About Us

    (this tool's focus is on) Flashcards included premade sets based off of both the HSK() and Integrated Chinese Textbooks. With us learn not only vocab but learn more about how culture relates. Learning culture along with vocab is important to better remember and understand the vocab in addition with connecting words to culutraly relevant ideas and terms. As you study after reviewing every Flashcard you have the opportunity to view the stroke order as well and practice it(this is functionality I need and hope to add). this will help you learn characters more effectively as well. You can also see your stats, what words you found hardest and easiest. This tool also has resources and strageties to help you memorize vocab becuase everyone learns differently!

    We hope this tool is useful to improve and increase your Chinese Language skills. We hope this helps you on your learning journey. Enjoy!
